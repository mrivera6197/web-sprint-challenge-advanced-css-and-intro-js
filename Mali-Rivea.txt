How would you describe acessibility on the web to someone new to programming?

acessibility is the process of making websites acessible to everyone, including those with auditory, cognitive, neurological or physical disabilites, slow internet connection, and/or visual needs. accessibility improves the website experience for everyone .  


Talk about 3 different things you can do to ensure your website is accessible.

- use responsive units (rems, percentages) to ensure the font-size/imgs/etc. will increase/decrease in response to the zoom-in/zoom-out settings
- use semantic HTML for screen readers to outline the page well
- use alt tags for images to describe what is in the image for those who may not be able to see
